A brief History

Saint John The Baptizer Parish

St. John The Baptizer Parish is Located at Tugbong, Kananga Leyte. It was founded, year 2017 on the 14th of February and is under the Vicariate of Ormoc. From a small barangay in Kananga Leyte and small chapel under Holy Family Parish in Kananga Leyte, St. Agustine Chapel of Tugbong was choosen to become a Mission Station to devide the chapels under the Parish of Kananga, and in the year 2010 St. Agustine Chapel of Tugbong Kananga, Leyte was change to St. John the Baptizer Mission Station with 8 chapels under it, with Rev. Fr. Irwin S. Gavilo as the Mission Station Priest for 5years and was change by Rev.Fr. Alejandro S. Abilido Jr. And after 2 years St. John The Baptizer Mission Station became a Parish last 2017 on the 14th of February.

Priest Currently Assigned

Rev. Fr. Alejandro "VOX" S. Abilido Jr.

Fr. Vox was assigned to St. John the Baptizer and he was also one of the reason why St. John the Baptizer Mission Station together with the other priest became a Parish. Fr. Vox was loved by it's Parishioners because of his kind attitude and and is also a supportive father to all the youths in the Parish.

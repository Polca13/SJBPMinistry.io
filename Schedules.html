Grand Aurora and March of Saints

The Official celebration for the Month of the Rosary and the feast of the Saints are the following:

Grand Aurora: 4:00 AM and Holy Mass 5:30 AM Our procession that day will be held in every chapels in parish. The procession will start by to each barangay's chapel and will end at the Parish. After the procession the Holy Mass will starts. Hoping that all the Parishioners will reach at the exact time given.

November 1, 2020

Arrival time 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM, procession starts at 4:30 PM after the Holy Mass will follow at 6:00PM Everyone should arrive at the time given so that the event will at exact time, Everyone is invited especially the Youths to attend and celebrate this event with us.

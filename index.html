October is the Month of the Rosary because we celebrate the memorial of Our Lady of the Rosary each year. The rosary is a living prayer form and continues to develop even in recent times. An invocation known as the Fatima Prayer was commonly added in the early 20th century. The Living Stations of the Cross is a passion play presented by the children of Every Parishes. It is a narration, along with music and acting. Above all, it is a prayer. Performances occur on the week prior to Holy Week. Holy Week is the annual Christian observance commemorating Jesus' last week on earth and the events leading up to his resurrection on Easter Sunday. It is the final week of Lent, and the week prior to Easter Sunday.
